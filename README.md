# 100 Days of Solana 🚀

Welcome to **100 Days of Solana**, a hands-on challenge series designed to take you from curiosity to capability. 

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Each day of this challenge represents a small, concrete step. Individually, they are manageable; together, they add up to a comprehensive, real-world understanding of the Solana ecosystem.

* **Hands-on Learning:** Focus on practical, daily tasks.
* **Skill Adaptation:** Bridge your current developer skills into Web3.
* **Architectural Insights:** Understand *why* to build on Solana and *what* makes it unique.
